Grilled Flounder

Description

Grilled flounder is a light and flavorful dish that's easy to prepare and perfect for any occasion. This delicate fish pairs well with a variety of seasonings and sides, making it a versatile choice for summer barbecues or cozy dinners.

Ingredients

  • 4 flounder fillets (about 6 ounces each)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice (freshly squeezed)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • Fresh parsley (for garnish)
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Zest of 1 lemon

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, paprika, salt, and black pepper.
  2. If using, add Dijon mustard, honey, and lemon zest for an extra kick.
  3. Place the flounder fillets in a shallow dish or a resealable plastic bag.
  4. Pour the marinade over the fish, making sure each fillet is well coated.
  5. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, up to 2 hours for more flavor.
  6. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat (about 375°F to 450°F). If using a charcoal grill, make sure the coals are hot and covered with ash.
  7. Remove the flounder from the marinade and let excess marinade drip off.
  8. Place the fillets on the grill, skin-side down (if applicable).
  9. Grill for about 3-4 minutes per side, or until the fish is opaque and flakes easily with a fork. Avoid overcooking.
  10. Remove the flounder from the grill and transfer to a serving platter. Garnish with fresh parsley and additional lemon wedges if desired.

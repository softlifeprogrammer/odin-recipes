Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a delicious Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, brown the ground beef. Drain fat.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta, egg, and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Spread some marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Layer noodles, meat, cheese mixture, and sauce. Repeat layers.
  7. Top with mozzarella cheese.
  8. Bake for 30-40 minutes until bubbly.
